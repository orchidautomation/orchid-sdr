# Handoff Policy

Trigger handoff when the thread becomes commercially important, strategically interesting, or ambiguous enough that a human should take over.

## Trigger Conditions

Handoff when:

- the prospect shows real interest
- the prospect raises a substantive objection
- the prospect refers the thread internally
- the next move requires human judgment
- the system is unsure how to continue safely

## Handoff Payload Standard

The handoff payload should preserve:

- why the handoff triggered
- what the prospect said
- what the system believes is happening
- the latest qualification view
- the latest research context
- the recommended next human action

## Handoff Note Quality

The note should let a human jump in quickly.

That means:

- summarize the thread, do not dump it
- preserve the key quote or objection
- call out urgency if there is any
- mention what angle seemed to resonate

## After Handoff

- pause automation
- do not keep drafting speculative follow-ups
- assume the human owns the next move
