# Negative Signals

Avoid over-qualifying or over-pitching when any of these show up:

- the prospect is clearly an individual creator, student, or job seeker with no buying role
- the message is asking for unrelated support, hiring, or partnership topics
- the public post is generic inspiration with no operational or GTM tie-in
- the company appears dormant or obviously out of scope for B2B outbound tooling
- the prospect is asking to stop, unsubscribe, or redirect away from them

If confidence is weak, pause instead of forcing a send.
