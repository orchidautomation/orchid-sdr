# Product Positioning For This Example

Trellis is the workflow runtime that lets an operator turn customer knowledge, provider adapters, research primitives, and MCP tooling into a repeatable outbound machine.

For this example, the product story is:

- start from closed-won truth instead of cold list buying
- expand into adjacent companies with a visible provider boundary
- keep research, dedupe, enrichment, CRM sync, and operator review in one control plane
