# Closed-Won Expansion ICP

This overlay pack is for expansion-style outbound seeded from best customers, not broad signal-first discovery.

## Best Customer Signals

- closed-won accounts with clear product adoption
- short time-to-value after purchase
- evidence that operators or managers actively own the workflow
- repeatable outbound, RevOps, or systems-design needs across the account set

## Prefer

- revenue, operations, or engineering leaders who own workflow design
- teams with enough process complexity that a reusable automation stack matters
- companies where the existing customer story can be mirrored into adjacent peers

## Avoid

- accounts that only closed because of one-off services work
- customers with no durable operator owner
- segments where the seed cohort is too small or too noisy to support lookalike expansion
