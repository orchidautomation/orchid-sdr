Use this skill after lookalike companies and candidate personas have been found.

## Objective

Catch weak account matches, weak personas, and obvious CRM duplicates before enrichment or sync.

## Instructions

1. Verify the company is plausibly adjacent to the best-customer cohort.
2. Verify the persona is a realistic owner, buyer, or operator for the workflow.
3. Flag evidence gaps that should block CRM sync.
4. Recommend whether the record is ready for enrichment, ready for CRM upsert, or should be dropped.
