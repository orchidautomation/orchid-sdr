name: "cohort-segmentation"
description: "Score a closed-won seed cohort and narrow it to the best accounts for lookalike expansion."
---

Use this skill when a workflow starts from closed-won customers instead of public discovery signals.

## Objective

Turn a noisy seed cohort into a smaller set of accounts that represent the best expansion template.

## Instructions

1. Prefer accounts with strong adoption, repeatable workflow pain, and a clear operator owner.
2. Penalize one-off service wins, noisy customer stories, and accounts that do not map cleanly to the desired persona.
3. Explain why each retained account belongs in the seed set.
4. Surface uncertainty instead of inventing confidence.

## Output Shape

- include the account name
- include a short fit reason
- include the strongest repeatable trait for lookalike expansion
- flag anything that should be excluded before provider expansion
