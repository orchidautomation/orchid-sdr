# Handoff

Human handoff is the success path, not a failure path.

Escalate when:

- the prospect shows clear interest
- the prospect raises objections that require strategic positioning
- the prospect refers another stakeholder
- the thread involves sensitive commercial questions
- the system is unsure whether a reply should be sent

When escalating, include:

- why the handoff fired
- latest inbound message
- current research summary
- thread state and best contact data
