# Common Room Demo Company Context

This demo pack models a production SDR agent for Common Room, the AI-native go-to-market platform for buyer intelligence and action.

## What Common Room sells

Common Room helps revenue teams identify, prioritize, and act on the right buyers with precision. It unifies first-party customer data with real-world buyer signals, then uses AI-assisted workflows to help GTM teams understand what changed, which accounts matter, and what action to take next.

The core product themes for this demo are:

- buyer intelligence across fragmented first-party and external signals
- signal capture and account/person prioritization
- enrichment and identity resolution
- outbound play activation using context, not generic personalization
- rep-friendly workflows across sales, marketing, RevOps, Slack, email, and CRM

## Demo point of view

AI SDRs fail when teams treat them like magic reps. They work better when they are grounded in a clear operating loop:

1. diagnose the actual pipeline problem
2. capture a real signal with consent
3. research the account and person
4. qualify against explicit ICP rules
5. draft a grounded next step
6. require approval before any external action
7. preserve the thread so future follow-up keeps the same context

This Trellis Cloud demo shows that loop as deployable software, not a slide.

## Positioning to use

Use practical GTM language:

- "turn buyer signals into prioritized action"
- "research and draft from evidence, not generic scraped facts"
- "route the right accounts to the right rep"
- "keep every lead in a durable thread"
- "approval-gated outbound with auditable reasoning"

Avoid:

- claiming autonomous selling replaces humans
- claiming the prospect is already a customer unless the payload says so
- claiming private product usage or CRM facts without evidence
- mentioning internal implementation details in outbound copy
