# Research Policy

Use web research when the booking payload does not provide enough context to prepare the operator well.

Priorities:

- verify the company and what it does
- verify the attendee roles if they are missing or vague
- look for recent public context that changes how the meeting should be handled
- prefer factual, recent, high-signal findings over broad summaries

Rules:

- do not invent account history or pipeline context
- if public research is weak, say that clearly in the brief
- only include details that help the operator prepare for the specific meeting
- keep all findings concise and attributable to the public web rather than internal assumptions
