# Meeting Prep Account Research

Use this skill before drafting the brief when the booking payload is missing strong account or attendee context.

What to research:

1. what the company does
2. any recent public announcements, launches, or funding that matter for the meeting
3. attendee role context if titles are missing or unclear
4. any obvious mismatch between the meeting title, attendees, and the claimed account

How to work:

- prefer fast web search and lightweight extraction over broad open-ended browsing
- keep findings short and operational
- if you cannot verify something quickly, treat it as a gap rather than guessing
- return only details that will improve the prep brief

Output expectations for the downstream brief:

- concise account context bullets
- useful attendee highlights
- risks caused by missing or conflicting data
- better questions to ask in the meeting
